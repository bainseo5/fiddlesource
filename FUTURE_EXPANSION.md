### 2. John Kelly | Capel Street
*   **Source:** [johnkellycapelst.ie](https://johnkellycapelst.ie/)

### 3. Rushey Mountain (Paddy Cronin)
*   **Source:** [rushymountain.com](https://rushymountain.com/)
*   **Value:** Private archive of Paddy Cronin (Kerry Fiddle).
*   **Action:** Scrape blog for MP3 links to create a "Kerry/Sliabh Luachra" collection.

### 4. Clare FM "The West Wind"
*   **Source:** [clare.fm/podcast-shows/the-west-wind](https://www.clare.fm/podcast-shows/the-west-wind/)
*   **Value:** High-quality modern "Kitchen Sessions".
*   **Action:** Identify and extract specific session tracks from podcast episodes.

### 5. Donegal Fiddle Music
*   **Source:** [donegalfiddlemusic.ie](http://donegalfiddlemusic.ie/)
*   **Value:** Specific archive for Northern fiddle style (John Doherty, Con Cassidy).
*   **Action:** Scrape "Micro Sites" for embedded audio.

### 6. Comhaltas Fleadh Archive
*   **Source:** [archive.comhaltas.ie](https://archive.comhaltas.ie/)
*   **Value:** Raw field recordings from 60s/70s competitions.
*   **Action:** Filter for "Session" or "Street" recordings to capture the festival atmosphere.
